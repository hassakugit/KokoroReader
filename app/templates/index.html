Markup Cheat Sheet

Use tags at the beginning of a paragraph/line to change parameters dynamically.

[voice:code]

Switches the narrator voice.

Ex: [voice:af_sarah] Hello there.

[speed:number]

Changes speech speed (0.5 - 2.0).

Ex: [speed:1.5] I am talking fast!

Dialogue Example:
[voice:am_adam] Hey Sarah, how are you? [voice:af_sarah][speed:1.2] I'm great Adam! Just moving a bit fast today. [voice:am_adam] No worries.
